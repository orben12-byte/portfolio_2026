Design that connects people to spaces and systems

I'm a Product Designer based in Haifa. I started in physical experience design — wayfinding, signage systems, spatial IA — and brought those principles into digital product work.

What I care about most: making complex systems legible. Whether it's a hospital with 800 signs or an app with 50 flows, the problem is the same — how do people know where they are and where to go next?

Currently at Raya Galboa Design Studio, previously at Shnorkel Studio (BDO Digital). Also teaching Figma to the next generation of designers.
